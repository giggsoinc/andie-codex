# Privacy Policy — Andie-Codex

**Last updated: May 12, 2026**
**Published by: Giggso Inc**

---

## What Andie-Codex Collects

Andie-Codex is a local instruction set. It does not collect, transmit, or store any data on Giggso-operated servers.

All conversations stay within your chosen platform (GitHub Copilot, OpenAI, or Claude Code). No data is sent to Giggso.

---

## Platform-Specific Privacy

**GitHub Copilot:** Governed by [GitHub's Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement) and [Microsoft's Privacy Policy](https://privacy.microsoft.com).

**OpenAI Custom GPT:** Governed by [OpenAI's Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy).

**Claude Code:** Governed by [Anthropic's Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy).

Andie-Codex adds no additional data collection beyond what each platform already does.

---

## What Andie-Codex Does Not Do

- Does not collect telemetry or usage data
- Does not phone home to Giggso servers
- Does not transmit your conversations externally
- Does not store session data
